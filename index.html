What we are doing...

We believe that “offense is the best driver for defense.”
The existing adversary lifecycle and Cyber Kill Chain concepts were too high-level to relate behaviors to defenses. The dynamic analysis such as API call sequence analysis is relatively low level. Nonetheless, they can show exactly what activities have been done by adversary. These two levels of abstraction must meet somewhere in the middle based on the true execution trace to derive the tactics, techniques and procedures (TTP) of a malicious program.
